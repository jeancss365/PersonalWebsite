關於我About Me

我是 Jean，台北商業大學文化創意產業系二年級學生。我熱衷於文化策展、策略行銷與品牌體驗設計之間的創意整合。

I'm Jean, a sophomore at National Taipei University of Business studying Culture Event Development. My passion lies at the intersection of cultural curation, strategic marketing, and brand experience design.

我擁有文化翻譯、觀眾行為研究與跨文化溝通經驗，致力於將深度文化洞察轉化為與受眾共鳴、產生社會價值的感官體驗。

With experience in cultural translation, audience behavior research, and cross-cultural communication, I believe in transforming deep cultural insights into sensory experiences that resonate with audiences and create social value.

我特別喜歡能跨越文化差異、重新定義傳統觀點，並透過創意敘事與策略規劃開啟新可能的專案。

I'm particularly drawn to projects that bridge cultural gaps, redefine traditional perceptions, and unlock new possibilities through creative storytelling and strategic planning.
